Here is how to make Icecream in 8 steps!

1. Set aside a bowl of ice

2. Mix milk, cream, sugar, and vanilla extract

3. Beat eggs

4. Add the milk mixture

5. Thicken custard

6. Strain custard

7. Move it to the icecream maker

8. Freeze the ingreedients
